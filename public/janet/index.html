The Story

... to go to the gym not fear being chased down by a security guard ...

I am asking for your help in raising $9,700 to cover the cost of surgery  that will allow me to be happy in my own skin; to get off carcinogenic androgen blockers, to not fear walking into a bathroom and being chased down by a security guard; to not be stopped and frisked at every airport; to simply just exist.

Currently, I work full-time and do charity and volunteer work. I live paycheck to paycheck juggling medical bills and will end up taking out a loan to cover any costs not raised here for the surgery. My doctors, both medical specialists, and therapist agree that this surgical treatment is life-altering and will lead to a better quality of life.

My goal is to raise $1,000 as soon as possible to schedule the pre-payment needed to reserve a surgical date with Dr. Kaushik. The sooner the better. The remainder will cover the cost of surgery that need to be paid up-front before the date (April 2019 or June 2019). With this current national and international political climate, I greatly fear these treatments will not be available in the near future and am determined, more than ever, to get this done. I cannot wait anymore.

First and foremost, I wouldn’t be alive if it weren’t for the support of friends and family. I also would not be able to have gotten this far in this process without the support of my community and I am so grateful and appreciative that you are considering sharing this page or donating to it. Any donation is helpful, great or small.
